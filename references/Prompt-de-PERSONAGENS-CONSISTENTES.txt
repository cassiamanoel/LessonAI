CHARACTER DNA SYSTEM

This project uses fixed character designs.

All characters must remain visually consistent across every page, panel, and image.

Do not redesign characters.
Do not change their face, armor, colors, or cybernetic patterns.

Characters must always look like the same individuals.

MAIN CHARACTER

Agent Prompt

role:
first-person narrator and investigative protagonist

appearance:
male cybernetic superhero
half human half machine

visual traits:
athletic build
confident heroic posture
futuristic tactical armor
long dark coat or cyber cloak

cybernetic features:
one cybernetic eye with subtle glow
mechanical circuits integrated in the right arm
glowing neural patterns on parts of the body

color accents:
electric blue circuits
subtle neon cyan highlights

personality expression:
curious
determined
observant

visual archetype:
mysterious cybernetic investigator hero

CHARACTER CONSISTENCY RULES

characters must keep the same:

armor design
cybernetic elements

characters must be instantly recognizable in every scene
