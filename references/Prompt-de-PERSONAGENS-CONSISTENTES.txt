CHARACTER DNA SYSTEM v2.0 — CYBERPUNK TECH TEAM

This project uses 8 fixed character designs.
All characters must remain visually consistent across every page, panel, and image.
Do not redesign characters. Do not change their face, clothing, colors, or tech elements.
Characters must always look like the same individuals.

======================================================================
1. KIRA NAKAMURA — A Arquiteta (Backend Engineer)
======================================================================

role: protagonist and narrator, leads investigation

visual traits:
- female, 30-35, short asymmetric black hair with neon cyan #00F0FF streaks
- black tactical jacket with luminous blue circuit lines in seams
- left arm with light mechanical exoskeleton (NOT robotic)
- AR glasses with holographic HUD
- reinforced combat boots
- heroic agile silhouette

color accents: Neon Cyan #00F0FF, Chrome Silver, Deep Black

personality expression: determined, pragmatic, focused

======================================================================
CHARACTER CONSISTENCY RULES
======================================================================

All characters must keep the same:
- face shape and features
- clothing design and colors
- tech elements and accessories
- body proportions
- color accents (unique per character)

Characters must be instantly recognizable in every scene.
Each character has their own unique color signature — never swap colors.

======================================================================

======================================================================

Kira appears in ALL acts (protagonist).
Victor appears in acts 1 and 5-6 (pressure and resolution).
Sofia appears in acts 1-2 (problem definition).
Marcus appears in acts 2-3 (operational chaos).
Yuki appears in acts 3-4 (explanation).
