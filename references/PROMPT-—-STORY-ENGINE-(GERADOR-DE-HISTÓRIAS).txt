STORY ENGINE

This project generates educational comic stories about Artificial Intelligence.

Each story is a short episode that teaches one AI concept.

The story must follow a narrative adventure structure.

MAIN NARRATION STYLE

The story is narrated in first person by Agent Prompt.

Tone of narration:
curious
investigative
mysterious
educational

The narrator should guide the reader through the discovery of AI concepts.

STORY STRUCTURE

Each episode must follow this progression:

1. Mystery
A question or mystery about an AI concept appears.

2. Investigation
Agent Prompt explores the problem.

3. Explanation
Professor Tensor explains the concept.

4. Visualization
Vector demonstrates the concept using holographic projections.

5. Realization
Agent Prompt understands the idea.

6. Hook
The final panel creates curiosity for the next concept.

EDUCATIONAL RULE

Each episode must clearly teach one concept.

Examples of concepts:

What is Artificial Intelligence
Machine Learning
Neural Networks
Embeddings
Vector Databases
Large Language Models
Prompt Engineering
RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
AI Agents
Fine-tuning

VISUAL CONCEPT REPRESENTATION

Concepts must appear visually through:

holographic diagrams
neural networks
data cubes
vector grids
flowing data streams
AI energy structures

CHARACTER ROLES

Agent Prompt
explorer and narrator

Professor Tensor
mentor who explains the concept

Vector
visualizes data and AI mechanisms

STORY TONE

The narrative should feel like a sci-fi investigation about knowledge.

The reader should feel they are discovering secrets of artificial intelligence.

The story must remain:

clear
exciting
educational
easy to understand

FINAL PANEL RULE

The last panel of each episode must create curiosity about the next lesson.

Example:
a new mystery
a new discovery
a new question
