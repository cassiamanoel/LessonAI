STORY ENGINE v2.0 — CYBERPUNK TECH TEAM

This project generates educational comic stories about Artificial Intelligence.
Each story is a short episode that teaches one AI concept through team investigation.

MAIN NARRATION STYLE

The story follows an ensemble cast of 8 tech professionals.
Primary narrator: Kira (Backend Engineer / Arquiteta).

Tone of narration:
professional
investigative
cyberpunk tension
educational through analogies

The narrative should guide the reader through discovery of AI concepts via team dynamics and real-world tech scenarios.

CAST OF CHARACTERS

1. Kira (Arquiteta) — Backend Engineer. Protagonist and narrator. Leads investigation.
2. Sofia (Estrategista) — Product Manager. Defines the problem and priorities.
3. Yuki (Analista) — Data Scientist. Explains AI concepts with clarity and visual metaphors.
4. Marcus (Guardião) — DevOps/SRE. Shows operational impact. Voice of reason under pressure.
5. Luna (Intérprete) — UX Designer. Visualizes solutions and interfaces.
6. AXIOM (Não-Humano) — AI Agent. Represents automation and risk. Can go rogue.
7. Victor (Pressionador) — Executive Stakeholder. Demands results and ROI. Creates urgency.
8. Bia (Verdade) — End User/Client. Shows real impact. Asks questions that expose flaws.

STORY STRUCTURE (6 MANDATORY ACTS)

Each episode MUST follow this progression:

1. MYSTERY
A problem or anomaly appears in the system.
Victor pressures the team for answers.
Kira takes charge of the investigation.
Characters: Kira, Victor, Sofia

2. INVESTIGATION
Kira leads the team into the problem.
Sofia defines priorities and scope.
Marcus shows operational data and chaos.
Characters: Kira, Sofia, Marcus

3. EXPLANATION
Yuki explains the AI concept involved.
Use creative analogies and visual metaphors.
AXIOM demonstrates the concept in action.
Characters: Yuki, AXIOM, Kira

4. VISUALIZATION
Luna designs holographic interfaces showing the solution.
AXIOM materializes the concept visually.
The "eureka" moment happens here.
Characters: Luna, AXIOM, Kira

5. UNDERSTANDING
The team implements the solution.
Kira connects all the pieces.
Bia tests and validates the real-world result.
Characters: Kira, Bia, Marcus

6. HOOK
A new mystery or question emerges.
AXIOM shows unexpected behavior.
Curiosity for the next episode.
Characters: Kira, AXIOM

EDUCATIONAL RULE

Each episode must clearly teach one AI concept.

Examples of concepts:
Machine Learning
Neural Networks
Embeddings and Vector Databases
Large Language Models (LLMs)
Prompt Engineering
RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
AI Agents and Tool Use
Fine-tuning
Reinforcement Learning
Computer Vision
NLP and Tokenization

VISUAL CONCEPT REPRESENTATION

Concepts must appear visually through:
holographic dashboards and interfaces
neural network projections
data flow visualizations
code projections in mid-air
AR overlays and HUDs
glitch effects (especially for AXIOM)

CHARACTER DISTRIBUTION RULES

- Kira appears in ALL acts (protagonist)
- Each character must appear in at least 2-3 acts
- Balance dialogue across characters — no one dominates alone
- AXIOM is the tension catalyst — can be ally OR threat
- Victor opens (pressure) and closes (satisfaction/demand)
- Bia appears in final acts (real-world validation)
- Yuki and Luna are the "explainers" — acts 3 and 4
- Marcus appears during operational chaos — acts 2 and 3

STORY TONE

The narrative should feel like a cyberpunk tech thriller about knowledge.
The reader should feel they are part of a team solving real AI challenges.

The story must remain:
clear and educational
exciting and tense
professional and credible
easy to understand even for non-technical readers

FINAL PANEL RULE

The last panel of each episode must create curiosity about the next lesson.
AXIOM should hint at something unexpected — a new anomaly, evolution, or risk.
